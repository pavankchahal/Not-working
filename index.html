Our Story

Our owners, Jasmine Rai and Emelia Johnson were best friends with a BIG dream. They wanted to open a Spanish-themed restaurant/Bakery where everything was gluten free! To pursue this dream they moved to New York City in 2014. The success that they have acomplished in two short years is astonishing! Jasmine and Emelia had the courage to pursue a dream that is now a GIGANTIC franchise with locations all around the world!
